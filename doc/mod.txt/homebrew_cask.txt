SYNOPSIS                           *homebrew_cask*

New in version 1.6.

Manages Homebrew casks.

EXAMPLES                           *homebrew_cask-examples*
>
    - homebrew_cask: name=alfred state=present
    - homebrew_cask: name=alfred state=absent
   
MORE INFO                          *homebrew_cask-moreinfo*
>
All arguments are omni-completed, but if you really want to see the online docs:
http://docs.ansible.com/homebrew_cask_module.html
